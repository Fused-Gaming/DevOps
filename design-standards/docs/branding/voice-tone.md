# Voice & Tone

VLN's communication style and brand personality.

## Voice Principles

- **Clear**: Simple, direct language
- **Professional**: Technical but approachable
- **Helpful**: Solution-oriented
- **Modern**: Contemporary without being trendy

## Tone Examples

### Success Messages
✅ "Deployment successful! Your changes are live."

### Errors
❌ "Deployment failed. Check logs for details."

### Info
ℹ️ "Deploying to production... This may take a few minutes."

## Writing Guidelines

- Use active voice
- Be concise
- Avoid jargon when possible
- Use emojis sparingly
